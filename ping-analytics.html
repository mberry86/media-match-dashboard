What is a Super Shopper?
A consumer who contacts Centerfield through 3+ distinct sources within a single AEP season AND reshops after a policy has already been submitted. Internal data confirms a direct correlation between Super Shopper behavior and lower effectuation rates and lower LTV. The signal is captured via Response Code 335 (Global Dupe Blocked) — a source with elevated 335 rate is a Super Shopper amplifier. Data scope: AEP 2023–2025 (Oct 15 – Dec 7).
